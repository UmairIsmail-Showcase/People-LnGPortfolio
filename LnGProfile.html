PEOPLE™ - a subsidiary company of the GSB Group, is one of the largest providers of Human Resource Services and Business Process Outsourcing across the Middle-East and South Central Asia.

Since its establishment in 2001, People™ has gained a reputation as the source of reliable and world-class services for clients across all industries in more than 20 countries.
